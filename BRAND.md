# Twakka Achar — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

Twakka Achar is a premium Nepali achar (pickle) brand offering authentic, handcrafted condiments with a rich cultural heritage.

---

## Color Palette

| Token            | Name             | Hex       | Usage                            |
|------------------|------------------|-----------|----------------------------------|
| `spice-gold`     | Spice Gold       | `#A88849` | Primary accent, CTAs, highlights |
| `spice-gold-dark`| Spice Gold Dark  | `#8a6e3a` | Hover states, borders            |
| `chilli-red`     | Chilli Red       | `#491612` | Navbar, footer, bold accents     |
| `cream`          | Cream            | `#F6F1E8` | Page background                  |
| `white`          | White            | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, surfaces                  |
| `dark-text`      | Dark Text        | `#1C1208` | Primary text                     |
| `muted-text`     | Muted Text       | `#6B5B45` | Secondary text, captions         |

---

## Typography

| Role      | Font             | Weights        | Usage                        |
|-----------|------------------|----------------|------------------------------|
| Headings  | Playfair Display | 400, 700, 900  | H1–H4, hero titles, labels   |
| Body      | Poppins          | 300, 400, 500, 600 | Body copy, UI text, buttons |

---

## Layout & Style

- **Navbar / Footer:** Dark Chilli Red (`#491612`) background, white text, gold accent links
- **Page Background:** Cream (`#F6F1E8`)
- **Cards / Surfaces:** White (`#FFFFFF`) with subtle shadow
- **CTA Buttons:** Spice Gold (`#A88849`) background, dark text or white text
- **Borders & Dividers:** Spice Gold Dark (`#8a6e3a`) at low opacity

---

## Design Principles

1. **Authentic & Premium** — evoke tradition and quality through warm tones and serif typography
2. **Bold but Inviting** — chilli red anchors the brand with energy; cream softens it
3. **Consistent Gold Accents** — gold is used sparingly to highlight key interactions
4. **Clean Hierarchy** — Playfair Display for display text, Poppins for readability
